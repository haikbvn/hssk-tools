# Sponsor QR images

`vietqr.png` and `momo.png` are **placeholders**. Replace them with your real payment QR codes
before release (keep the filenames).

Export tips:
- **VietQR** — generate at https://vietqr.io (download as PNG, ~600×600).
- **MoMo** — open your MoMo app → "Receive" → screenshot or export the QR at full resolution.

Both files are bundled into the frozen macOS/Windows app via `packaging/hssk_gui.spec`.
